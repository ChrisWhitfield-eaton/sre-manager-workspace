0. PURPOSE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Establish a world‑class, no‑slides Weekly Business Review (WBR) that drives
Eaton’s software culture toward transparency, speed, and predictable delivery.
One mechanism, one truth: weekly performance, decisions, and commitments
from system‑of‑record data; aligned to Eaton’s Hoshin objectives.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. OPERATING PRINCIPLES
----------------------------------------------------------------------
1) One plan, one team; facts & data over opinion; transparency & accountability.
2) Exception‑only discussion: reds and emerging ambers; no “green talk.”
3) Help‑needed is expected; no blame.
4) Decisions captured live; commitments have owners and dates.
5) No slide theatre: auto‑generated pre‑reads from system‑of‑record data.
6) Time discipline: agenda and slots enforced; silence = agreement.

RAG Definitions
- RED: >10% milestone slip OR SLO breach OR error‑budget exhausted OR guardrail/aging breach
- AMBER: 5–10% forecasted slip OR rising risk likely to breach within 2 weeks
- GREEN: within plan and within thresholds

   - Guardrails (auto‑displayed weekly):
     • DCI ≥ 90% (weekly committed items shipped)
     • DORA mini‑set + PR review latency < 48h
     • Flow Efficiency trend & WIP compliance
     • Platform leverage (standards adoption %, reuse index, EELD compliance %)
     • Quality hygiene (escaped defects; aging PRs/stories/incidents)
     • AGING RADAR: PRs > 7 days; stories > 30 days; incidents > 7 days; P1 defects > 72h; actions > 14 days overdue
   - Auto‑promotion: any guardrail/aging breach → 2–3 min exception slot
   - Rotation: one Tier‑B team does a 90‑sec “what changed?” spotlight weekly

7. RELIABILITY & READINESS GOVERNANCE (TWO LANES)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Lane A — Cloud & Applications
   - SRE SLOs & error‑budget policy; if budget exhausted: freeze/slow releases except P0/security until SLO restored
   - Weekly: DF, LTc, CFR, FDRT, SLO %, adopter proxies (MAU, API latency/success, SDK success)

Lane B — Embedded & Safety‑Critical
   - Release readiness gates (traceability, verification coverage, compliance incl. ASPICE/ITAR)
   - Default PR review latency < 48h
   - Exception policy: if audit/compliance or controlled technical data constraints make <48h impractical,
     the Program Manager records the exception in WBR, provides a remediation date, and reports weekly until back within target
   - Weekly: on‑time deliverables; verification progress; defect containment; compliance actions; PR latency; Flow Efficiency
